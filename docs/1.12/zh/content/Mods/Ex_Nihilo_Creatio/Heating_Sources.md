
## 使用方式

- **[IItemStack](/Vanilla/Items/IItemStack/) input** The block that supplies heat.
- **Int heat** heat per instance.

## 添加配方

```zenscript

mods.exnihilocreatio.Heat.addRecipe(<minecraft:log>, 20);
```
